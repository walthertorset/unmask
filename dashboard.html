To compare hotels, you need to:

  1. Install the Unmask Chrome Extension (if you haven't already)
  2. Visit Booking.com and find hotels you're interested in
  3. Click "Analyze with Unmask" on each hotel page
  4. Return here to see your analyzed hotels and compare them side-by-side

Hotels will automatically appear in your library below once analyzed.
