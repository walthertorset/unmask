How It Works

Unmask runs quietly in your browser, automatically analyzing reviews when you visit hotel booking sites.

1

Install Extension

Add our Chrome extension to your browser with a single click. It's free and lightweight.

2

Browse Hotels

Visit Booking.com and browse as normal. Our AI analyzes thousands of reviews in the background.

3

See True Scores

We reveal the "True Score" next to the listed rating, flagging inflated scores and fake reviews.

4

Dashboard

Save interesting hotels to your personal Dashboard to compare them side-by-side.

About Unmask

Unmask uses advanced AI analysis to reveal the true quality of hotels by analyzing thousands of reviews. Our Chrome extension calculates genuine review scores, helping you book hotels with confidence and avoid misleading ratings.

We believe in transparency and empowering travelers with accurate information to make the best accommodation choices.
